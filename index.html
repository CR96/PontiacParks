About this project

This project supplements research performed in the summer of 2017 as part of Health Science 405: Community Engaged Research Training, a course offered through the School of Health Sciences at Oakland University.

Equipment used

Images and geolocation data for this project were collected with a Garmin Oregon 650t and organized using Google Earth Pro 7.3.
